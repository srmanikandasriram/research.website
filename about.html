Hey! I am Mani, born and brought up in Chennai, India and I've pretty much spent my entire life in this city. My passion defines me and drives me. I do what I like to do and I do it right! Robotics and Web development have been the two major sides to my tech life so far. During other times, I like to dance, drive and dream.

I am currently in the pre-final year of my 5-year Dual Degree (B.Tech & M.Tech) program in Electrical Engineering department at Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras. I am currently working under Prof. Bharath Bhikkaji on formation control for multi-robot systems built using Lego Mindstorms® kits. A slightly longer about me +

I am fascinated by Intelligent Robots. My first experience with embedded systems was in my first year in college when my hostel seniors introduced me to Atmega 16 micro-controller. Since then, my enthusiasm for control, automation, robotics have been constantly increasing. Having spent three years of my college taking part in competitions and working on various projects, I can now tell with confidence that I am no newbie to this field. At the same time, I am aware that Robotics is a highly multi-disciplinary field which is still in its youth. I am excited to work in this field that I am deeply passionate about.

I love physics and in particular, Astrophysics. I still get goosebumps thinking about the vastness that lies beyond our perceivable senses. If you have read The Universe in a Nutshell by Stephen Hawking, you would be able to relate to this. It is a beautifully written text which is a must-read for anyone who is curious about the Space!
